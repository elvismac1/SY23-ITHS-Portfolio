Discover the sea that never sleeps in the waters right across the boardwalk. Or take in one of Coney Island's best shows at the Aquatheater. Take in a sea lion show at the Aquatheater, chill out while zebra sharks swim overhead, or get to know some of the ocean's oddest named residents (striped pajama squid, anyone?).
